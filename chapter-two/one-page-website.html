Overview

"Honkai Star Rail" follows the Trailblazer aboard the Astral Express, a cosmic train traversing the universe via the "Star Rail." The adventure begins with the Herta Space Station's attack by the Antimatter Legion, where the Trailblazer awakens with the Stellaron, an artifact, joining forces with March 7th and Dan Heng. As they journey across planets, they confront various factions and adversaries, unraveling the mystery of their origins and the significance of the Stellaron. Allies are made, enemies are faced, and secrets are uncovered, culminating in a quest that leads them to Penacony, where political intrigue and dreamscape mysteries await.

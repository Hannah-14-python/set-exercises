Puto Cheese

Puto, a Filipino delicacy, is a steamed rice cake that's enjoyed as a quick bite or breakfast treat. Its preparation is simple, calling for only a handful of ingredients. Traditionally, it's paired with shredded coconut and a touch of butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons of baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ¾ cup of sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups of fresh milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese (for topping)

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into greased muffin cups, filling each cup about ¾ full.
  5. Sprinkle the grated cheese on top of each puto.
  6. Steam the muffins for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the puto comes out clean.
